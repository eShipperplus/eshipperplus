How to install on your scanner device

1

Log in with your account credentials
4

5
Done! The app icon will appear on your home screen — no URL needed next time
Zebra / Honeywell / other scanner devices: Use the built-in browser or install Chrome from the Play Store, then follow the steps above. Once the icon is on your home screen you never need to type the URL again.
